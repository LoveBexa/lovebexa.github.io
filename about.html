Hi, I'm Becky a Writer, Designer and Developer.

View Resume    View Projects

Experience within eCommerce, gaming and adTech industries. Currently studying for a Masters in Computer Science and holds a Bachelors in Graphic Design.

My dream job growing up was to become an Inventor because I wanted to create something from nothing instead I ended up building very terrible websites on Neopets and Angelfire. Fast forward 20 years I realised creativity was in some sense the invention of new ideas so I worked as a Designer for 8 years.
Further along my career I saw how important data was in design decisions, how important design was to development and how important development there is in data. Each subject held a very similar iterative process. They also fed onto eachother and were interconnected. Therefore I made it my interest to bring these skills together to help others. I am a lifelong learner with the thirst for knowledge and the interest in combining disparate skillsets to solve problems.
